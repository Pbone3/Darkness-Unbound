Darkness Unbound is a HUGE content mod that makes the game, harder, more fun, longer, bigger, and stronger, too.
It features:
 - 1 boss
 - X items
 - X npcs
 - Throwing class!
 - 1 biome
 - 1 new difficulty mode: Restless Shadows
 - 1 new coin: Hellstone Coin
